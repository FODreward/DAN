1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies contain information about your browsing activity and preferences, allowing websites to remember your settings and provide a more personalized experience. They cannot access other files on your device or install malicious software.

AdChain Pro uses cookies to enhance your experience on our platform, deliver targeted advertisements, and analyze usage patterns to improve our services.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 By Duration

Session Cookies

These temporary cookies are deleted when you close your browser. They help maintain your session while navigating our platform and ensure security during your visit.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until you delete them. They remember your preferences and settings for future visits.

You can manage your cookie preferences using our Cookie Preference Center, accessible through the cookie banner or your account settings. This allows you to:

  • Accept or reject different categories of cookies
  • View detailed information about each cookie
  • Update your preferences at any time
  • Understand the impact of your choices

5.3 Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can manage cookies through your browser app settings or device privacy settings. Some mobile browsers also offer private browsing modes that don't store cookies.

5.4 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Platform may not function properly
  • Functional cookies: Settings and preferences won't be remembered
  • Analytics cookies: We can't improve our services based on usage data
  • Advertising cookies: You may see less relevant advertisements
