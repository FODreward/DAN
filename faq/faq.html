General Questions

What is AdChain Pro?

AdChain Pro is a premium ad viewing platform that connects users with high-quality advertisements from top brands worldwide. Users earn points by viewing relevant ads, which can be redeemed for cash, gift cards, and other valuable rewards. We create a win-win situation where advertisers reach engaged audiences and users are fairly compensated for their time and attention.

How does AdChain Pro work?

The process is simple:

  1. Sign up for a free account and choose your preferred reward plan
  2. Complete your profile to receive targeted advertisements
  3. View ads daily and earn points based on your engagement
  4. Redeem your points for cash, gift cards, or other rewards

Our advanced targeting system ensures you see ads that are relevant to your interests, making the experience more enjoyable and rewarding.

Is AdChain Pro free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and starting to earn is completely free. New users receive a $25 welcome bonus upon registration. While we offer premium plans that provide enhanced earning opportunities, you can begin earning immediately with our free tier. There are no hidden fees, setup costs, or mandatory purchases.

What types of ads will I see?

We partner with premium brands to show high-quality advertisements including:

  • Video advertisements from major brands
  • Interactive product demonstrations
  • Survey and feedback campaigns
  • New product launches and announcements
  • Educational and informational content

All ads are carefully curated to ensure they're relevant, engaging, and from reputable companies.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. We take privacy and security very seriously. Your personal information is protected by enterprise-grade encryption and security measures. We never sell your personal data to third parties. We only use your information to provide better ad targeting and improve your experience on our platform. You can review our complete privacy policy for detailed information about how we handle your data.

Account Management

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on our homepage
  2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
  3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
  4. Complete your profile with basic demographic information
  5. Choose your preferred reward plan
  6. Start viewing ads and earning rewards immediately

The entire process takes less than 5 minutes, and you'll receive your $25 welcome bonus upon completion.

Can I change my reward plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your reward plan at any time from your account dashboard. Plan changes take effect immediately, and you'll be charged or credited the prorated difference. There are no penalties for changing plans, and you can switch as often as needed to find the plan that best fits your viewing habits and earning goals.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings" in your dashboard
  3. Click "Edit Profile"
  4. Update your information and preferences
  5. Save your changes

Keeping your profile updated helps us show you more relevant ads and can increase your earning potential.

What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, simply click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a secure reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No, each person is allowed only one AdChain Pro account. Multiple accounts violate our terms of service and can result in account suspension. We use advanced fraud detection systems to identify duplicate accounts. If you need to change your email address or other account details, please contact our support team for assistance.

Earnings & Rewards

How much can I earn?

Earnings vary based on several factors:

  • Plan Type: Basic ($50-100/month), Premium ($150-300/month), VIP ($400-800/month)
  • Activity Level: More consistent viewing leads to higher earnings
  • Engagement Quality: Completing full ads and surveys earns more points
  • Referrals: Earn 5-15% commission on referred users' earnings
  • Bonus Campaigns: Special promotions can significantly boost earnings

Most active users earn between $50-200 per month, with top performers earning $500+ monthly.

How are points calculated?

Points are calculated based on:

  • Ad Completion: Base points for watching full advertisements
  • Engagement Level: Bonus points for interacting with ads
  • Ad Type: Premium content offers higher point values
  • Plan Multiplier: Premium plans include earning multipliers
  • Consistency Bonus: Daily viewing streaks earn bonus points

Your dashboard shows detailed breakdowns of how points are earned for each activity.

What can I redeem points for?

Points can be redeemed for various rewards:

  • Cash: Direct PayPal or bank transfer
  • Gift Cards: Amazon, iTunes, Google Play, Steam, and 100+ retailers
  • Cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other popular coins
  • Charitable Donations: Donate to your favorite charities
  • Premium Subscriptions: Netflix, Spotify, and other services
  • Physical Products: Electronics, gadgets, and merchandise

New redemption options are added regularly based on user requests.

Do points expire?

Points do not expire as long as your account remains active. We consider an account active if you log in and view at least one ad within a 90-day period. If your account becomes inactive for more than 90 days, you'll receive email reminders before any points are affected. Reactivating your account within 180 days will restore all your points.

How does the referral program work?

Our referral program offers generous commissions:

  • Bronze (1-50 referrals): 5% commission
  • Silver (51-200 referrals): 8% commission
  • Gold (201-500 referrals): 12% commission
  • Platinum (500+ referrals): 15% commission

You earn commissions on all earnings generated by your referrals for as long as they remain active. Share your unique referral link through social media, email, or your website to start earning.

Technical Support

What devices and browsers are supported?

AdChain Pro works on all modern devices and browsers:

  • Desktop: Windows, Mac, Linux
  • Mobile: iOS, Android
  • Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
  • Requirements: JavaScript enabled, cookies allowed

No app download is required - everything works through your web browser for maximum convenience and security.

Why aren't ads loading?

If ads aren't loading, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Disable ad blockers and browser extensions
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Try a different browser or device
  5. Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  6. Refresh the page and try again

If problems persist, contact our technical support team for personalized assistance.

Can I use a VPN?

While VPNs aren't prohibited, they may affect your ad viewing experience. Our targeting system relies on accurate location data to show relevant advertisements. Using a VPN might result in fewer available ads or lower-quality targeting. For the best experience and maximum earnings, we recommend using your regular internet connection without a VPN.
