Corporate Governance

Board Oversight

Our Board of Directors includes independent members with expertise in compliance, risk management, and corporate governance. The board provides oversight of our compliance program and ensures accountability at the highest levels.

Compliance Committee

Our dedicated Compliance Committee, comprised of senior executives and legal experts, meets monthly to review compliance matters, assess risks, and ensure adherence to all applicable regulations.

Ethics Program

We maintain a comprehensive ethics program that includes a code of conduct, regular training, and confidential reporting mechanisms for employees and stakeholders to report concerns.

Risk Management

Our enterprise risk management framework identifies, assesses, and mitigates compliance risks across all business operations, with regular reporting to senior management and the board.

Regulatory Relationships

Proactive Engagement

We maintain proactive relationships with regulatory bodies and industry organizations to stay informed of evolving requirements and best practices:

Regulatory Reporting
